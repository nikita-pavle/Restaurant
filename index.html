Our History

Founded in 1965.FoodStop is a family-owned and operated restaurant that started as a food truck in the area in 2018.The owner's philosophy is to provide customers with excellent food, drinks, and exceptional service that makes them feel right at home.FoodStop has been a popular food destination since its inception, and it has become a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

The restaurant's menu is a reflection of the owners' passion for food and their desire to share that passion with others . They wanted to bring that same level of variety and quality to their hometown, and thus, FoodStop was born

FoodStop's popularity has grown steadily over the years, thanks to its delicious food and welcoming atmosphere. The restaurant has become a hub for the local community, where people can come together to enjoy great food and good company. FoodStop has also been featured in several local publications, including the Borivali Times and the Maharashtra Gazette. The restaurant's success has allowed the owners to expand their business, and they now operate several locations throughout the city

In addition to its excellent food and service, FoodStop is also committed to giving back to the community. The restaurant regularly donates a portion of its profits to local charities and organizations. The Owners believe that it's important to support the community that has supported them, and they are proud to be able to make a positive impact on the lives of others
